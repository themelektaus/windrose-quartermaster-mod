Custom Ship Shanties

Replace any of the 10 vanilla sea-shanties the crew sings on board (Cutter, Brig, Ketch, Frigate; player or crew-only). A replacement applies to all ship sizes automatically.

Drop a .wav file per slot - Quartermaster encodes it to Bink Audio at build time and splices it into a SoundWave template. No UE5 Editor cook needed.

Format: 44.1 kHz, stereo, 16-bit PCM. Convert other formats first with Audacity or ffmpeg (ffmpeg -i in.mp3 -ar 44100 -ac 2 -sample_fmt s16 out.wav).

Vanilla Shanty Slots

Click Browse... to pick a .wav file for a shanty slot. Click Reset to drop the override and fall back to vanilla.
